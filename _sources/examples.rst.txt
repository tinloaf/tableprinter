Examples
========

All examples are also available as Jupyter notebooks in the `examples` subfolder of the source.

The :ref:`first simple example <_example_simple>` shows you the bare minimum you need to know to use the Tableprinter.
In the :ref:`example with hierarchic data <_example_hierarchic>` you learn how you can pretty-print data that has
more than two dimensions.
